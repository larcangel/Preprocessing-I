About

Lisa is based out of Denver, Co. She spent her early days on Maui, Hi. She has a bachelor's degree in business entrepreneurship and management and associates in culinary. She is currently a UX/UI student at Lambda school.
Lisa was a business owner of a Crossfit gym in Denver, Co and a chef from coast to coast. She is a natural team player and values collaboration but a workhorse on solo projects.
She has always felt happy in her career and achieved everything she wanted from it but wanted more. She has a desire for growth and expansion regardless of temporary discomfort. She’s comfortable with being uncomfortable.
Curiosity in design brought her into this new passion. An area for growth and a display of her creativity and love for new challenges.
Outside of design, Lisa is a Crossfit and a nutrition coach. She finds inspiration in helping others reach their goals. She’s always finding new ways to be better, a better designer, a better friend, a better person.
